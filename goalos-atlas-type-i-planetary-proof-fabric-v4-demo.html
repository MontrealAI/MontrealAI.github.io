GoalOS scales by keeping raw intelligence and evidence local, authorizing work inside independently replicated cells, and propagating only compact proof commitments through regional, zone, and planetary root layers. Mission traffic grows with cells; the global root never processes every mission.

Type I design law: no single database, validator set, government, cloud, or chain sits on the critical path for every mission. Local cells continue safely during wide-area partitions; global roots provide asynchronous lineage and interoperability.
