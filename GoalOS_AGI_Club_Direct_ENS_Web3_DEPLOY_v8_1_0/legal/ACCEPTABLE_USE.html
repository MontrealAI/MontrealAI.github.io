Acceptable Use Policy

Lawful, authorized, evidence-bound use of the public preview and member institution.

Important. This document is production-oriented drafting, not a substitute for deployment-specific advice from qualified counsel or accessibility, security, privacy, tax, employment, or regulatory professionals.

Lawful and authorized use

Use GoalOS only for lawful objectives and only where you have authority over the data, systems, accounts, communications, intellectual property, and decisions involved.

Prohibited conduct

  • Fraud, impersonation, phishing, credential theft, malware, harassment, exploitation, or rights violations.
  • Unauthorized access, scraping, surveillance, data brokerage, spam, or deceptive commercial communication.
  • Uploading sensitive personal, privileged, classified, export-controlled, confidential customer, or trade-secret information to public/static surfaces.
  • Misrepresenting synthetic outputs, generated testimonials, forecasts, benchmarks, customers, payments, or results as real evidence.
  • Circumventing membership, licence, security, capability, or proof restrictions.

High-impact decisions

Do not use GoalOS as the sole basis for medical, legal, financial, employment, credit, insurance, public-safety, security, or other high-impact decisions. Obtain qualified human review and any required approvals.
