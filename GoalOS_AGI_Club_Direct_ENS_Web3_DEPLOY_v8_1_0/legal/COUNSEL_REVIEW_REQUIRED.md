# Counsel review required before production activation

This package contains production-oriented legal notices and clickwrap drafting, but it is not legal advice and cannot guarantee legal protection. Before launch, the responsible operator should obtain qualified review for at least: Québec language and privacy law, PIPEDA/other privacy regimes, consumer and contract law, CASL and marketing, intellectual property, sanctions/export controls, tax, employment, professional services, token/financial regulation, insurance, limitation-of-liability enforceability, accessibility, and every jurisdiction in which the utility or paid services are offered.

The operator must identify the actual legal entity, business address, privacy responsible person, service providers, processing activities, retention, security controls, incident process, and contracting model. Replace generic references only after counsel approves them.
