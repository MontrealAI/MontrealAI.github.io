GoalOS · proof-gated autonomous work

Drafts do not become memory.
Proof does.

GoalOS treats the LLM as the drafting and reasoning layer, not the authority layer. When the LLM reaches the edge of reliable knowledge, GoalOS creates proof debt. Proof debt becomes AGI Jobs. AGI Jobs return ProofBundles. ProofBundles update the Evidence Docket. Only validated results can enter Chronicle memory or influence future missions.
