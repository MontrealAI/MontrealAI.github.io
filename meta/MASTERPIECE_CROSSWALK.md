# Masterpiece Crosswalk

The Masterpiece Edition does not replace the source editions. It gives each one a precise institutional role.

| Source edition | Distinctive strength retained | Masterpiece use |
|---|---|---|
| **Grand Edition** | Complete curriculum, Mission Lab, AI Council, stress theatre, RSI frontier, Merkle lab, diligence vault, Deal Architect, Fieldbook, facilitator manual, casebook and prompt library | Deep workshop and institution-design environment |
| **APEX Boardroom** | High-end executive theatre, premium visual stagecraft, strategic urgency and short-form partner close | 90-second, 12-minute and 45-minute executive routes |
| **Sovereign v2** | 18 role-adaptive modules, REAL-001, first-party replay, Mission 1 → Mission 2, evidence maturity, ECI, Move-37, governed RSI and 30-day pilot architecture | Evidence and recursive-improvement authority lane |
| **Production Showcase v2.1** | Live partnership mission, Docket, Governed Decision State, commercial ladder, bounded pilot and proportionate participation receipt | Fastest commercial conversion route |
| **Grand Canonical research corpus** | Mission OS, AEP-001, PROOF_LOOPS, Chronicle, Validated Skill Graph, Merkle state, privacy, AGI Nodes, `$AGIALPHA`, RSI and AGI ALPHA | Canonical doctrine and technical source lineage |

## Synthesis decisions

1. **Four front doors, one launcher.** The user chooses the right velocity rather than navigating a monolith.
2. **Evidence before theatre.** First-party, deterministic-reference and illustrative lanes are visually distinct.
3. **Mission 2 is central.** Capability compounding is tested on fresh work under equal constraints.
4. **RSI is governed.** Novelty may allocate search; it cannot approve promotion.
5. **The customer wedge remains concrete.** The first product is one bounded Proof Mission, not wholesale adoption of the long-range architecture.
6. **Participation is proportionate.** The calibrated v2.1 hold-harmless and non-joinder wording is preserved.
7. **Source editions remain independently usable.** No original high-value artifact was discarded.
