Most important practical warning

•Trial submissions are public once sent on-chain. The contract stores trialURI and emits TrialSubmitted. If the URI points to public IPFS / HTTP content, competing finalists can read it.
•If you want less copy risk, submit later in the trial window, or use access-controlled / encrypted off-chain content. The URI string itself will still be public.

•No transaction is needed at Apr 6, Apr 13, or Apr 14 just to change the label. Time changes the label automatically.
Simple rule: the first real deadline that benefits from a backstop write is after Apr 15, when someone can call finalizeWinner(0) or advanceProcurement(0).
