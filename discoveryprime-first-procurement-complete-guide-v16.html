Finalization

How the remaining settlement steps work.

This section explains when finalizeJob(0) can succeed, what the contract will do, and how to send it in the safest, least confusing way.
Checking whether finalization is likely to be available…
The page is comparing the live vote picture with the review window and the challenge-after-approval rule.

Fast approval path

If approvals reach the required threshold and still beat disapprovals, the contract starts the challenge-after-approval timer. Once that timer expires, the fast approval path can complete the job if approvals still lead.

Review-end path

If the fast path does not already settle the job, someone can try finalizeJob(0) after the full review period. The contract then decides among agent-win, employer-win, or dispute.

No-vote path

If the review period ends and there were zero votes, the contract can still complete the job on the agent side — but under the weaker reputation path in code.

Dispute path

If disapprovals reach their threshold early, or if the review ends with a tie or sub-quorum result, the contract can push the job into dispute instead of directly settling it.

After finalization

What the contract does after the last write lands.

If the agent wins

AGIJobManagerPrime sends the agent-side payout according to the snapshotted payout percentage, settles validators automatically, mints the completion NFT to the employer, and marks the job completed.

If the employer wins

AGIJobManagerPrime sends the employer refund path, settles validators under the employer-win rules, and closes the job.

If the job moves into dispute

The job is not settled immediately. It enters the dispute path, where moderator or owner-driven resolution methods become relevant.

DiscoveryPrime money is separate

DiscoveryPrime claimable balances are still pull-based. If finalists, validators, or the employer have DiscoveryPrime claimable balances, they still need to call claim() on DiscoveryPrime separately. That is different from ManagerPrime’s automatic settlement transfers.
