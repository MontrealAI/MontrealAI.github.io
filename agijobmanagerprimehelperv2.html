Stage 1
Strategos / employer

Posting

createPremiumJobWithDiscovery(...)

Atomic ManagerPrime settlement creation + DiscoveryPrime procurement creation.

Capital / evidence flow Employer must approve payout escrow to ManagerPrime and the discovery budget to DiscoveryPrime before posting.

Stage 2
Applicant

Commitment

commitApplication(...)

Sealed entry with the 30 bps application stake.

Capital / evidence flow Small admission stake deters spam without pricing out challengers.

Stage 3
Applicant

Reveal

revealApplication(...)

Visible application and stake recovery logic.

Capital / evidence flow Properly revealed but unshortlisted applicants recover stake; unrevealed commitments can forfeit.

Stage 4
Strategos / operator

Shortlisting

finalizeShortlist(...) / advanceProcurement(...)

Moves exactly the configured number of finalists forward.

Capital / evidence flow History governs shortlist entry; no stipend is paid yet.

Stage 5
Finalist

Trials

acceptFinalist(...) + submitTrial(...)

Accepting may require a top-up to the finalist stake total; trial submission unlocks serious scoring.

Capital / evidence flow Paid work plus stake return make serious finalist participation economically worthwhile.

Stage 6
Validator

Validator scoring

commitFinalistScore(...) + revealFinalistScore(...)

Validators score finalists with a separate commit / reveal and a score-bond.

Capital / evidence flow The score-bond materially exceeds the per-reveal reward, so careful scoring matters.

Stage 7
Strategos / operator

Designation

finalizeWinner(...) / promoteFallbackFinalist(...)

The weighted composite decides the winner; fallback promotion exists if the designated winner stalls.

Capital / evidence flow Trial quality dominates final designation when quorum exists.

Stage 8
Designated winner + settlement validators

Handoff to ManagerPrime

applyForJob(...) + submitCheckpoint(...) + requestJobCompletion(...) + validateJob(...)

Designation is not payout; the selected agent must still claim the slot, post the ManagerPrime bond, submit checkpoint and completion, and pass settlement.

Capital / evidence flow Final settlement-side payout share depends on current ManagerPrime configuration at assignment time.

Best-practice reminders

  1. Use the same wallet for compute, approve, commit, reveal, and the later ManagerPrime handoff unless you intentionally intend a different actor to perform a different role.
  2. Do not change the applicant URI after you commit. Reveal must reuse the exact same URI and salt.
  3. Do not lose either salt. Save them in at least two places.
  4. If MetaMask shows a pending transaction, wait for it to confirm before sending the next one.
  5. If a transaction is likely to fail, read the blockers shown here instead of guessing.
