Public curation is a control. Exclusion does not imply impropriety; it means the public reference surface is not the correct authority or disclosure channel.

Excluded public-reference functions

  • autonomous external execution;
  • custody, payment, lending, brokerage, exchange, escrow or money transmission;
  • professional advice or representation;
  • high-impact decisions about people;
  • sensitive, privileged, export-controlled or customer data;
  • public power or critical-infrastructure control;
  • dual-use deployment without separate authorization;
  • filing, signing, contracting, procurement commitment or programme submission;
  • claims of guaranteed compliance, immunity, funding, value or return.

Withheld from public distribution

  • deployment-specific privileged legal, tax, securities, employment, insurance, sanctions and privacy work;
  • credentials, keys, recovery routes, private endpoints and exploit-sensitive details;
  • customer, partner, acquisition, investment, licensing and negotiation materials;
  • internal valuation, treasury, pricing and controlled operating books where publication increases risk.
