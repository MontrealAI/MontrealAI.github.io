Scope & Authority

Planning institution only. GoalOS composes candidate architectures and bounded work specifications. It does not form entities, make filings, apply for funding, hire workers, provide professional advice, execute contracts, move money or exercise external authority.

Covered mission classes

Companies, nonprofits, research programmes, investment funds, international expansions, infrastructure projects, industrial facilities and economic-development initiatives.

Evidence ladder

  1. Designed
  2. Validated
  3. Authorized
  4. In execution
  5. Completed
  6. Realized
  7. Monitored

Binding-action gate

No recommendation becomes an authorized transaction until current official sources, verified facts, qualified professional review, decision-maker authority, implementation controls and Chronicle receipts are complete.
