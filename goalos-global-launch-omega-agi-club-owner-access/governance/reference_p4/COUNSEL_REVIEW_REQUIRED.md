# Counsel Review Required

This release is a publication candidate, not a legal opinion. Before public deployment, the responsible publisher/operator should obtain jurisdiction-appropriate review of ownership, licensing, consumer terms, French-language delivery, privacy, securities and token communications, tax, sanctions/export controls, accessibility, limitation clauses, dispute provisions, and the actual facts of control and operation. Mandatory law, regulators, non-signatories, and actual conduct are not displaced by website terms.
