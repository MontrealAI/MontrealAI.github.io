Build the Institution

The founding act is not a token purchase or a slogan. It is one real mission under independent observation.

Four public participation paths

Nominate a mission

One consequential but reversible objective, one accountable sponsor, one deadline, one rollback.

Become an evaluator

Contribute held-out testing, red-team review, replay, conflict disclosure, and dissent.

Contribute standards

Help define thresholds, benchmark lifecycle, reviewer independence, and post-release response.

Operate a domain institution

Adopt the Charter, Portfolio Constitution, and Institutional Operating System for a bounded domain.

Freeze the objective. Protect the tests. Produce the Evidence Docket. Let proof determine the next authority.

Status boundary

Independent research and institutional architecture. No governmental, regulatory, accreditation, certification, investment, or token-sale status is claimed. Official authority requires separate lawful designation and evidence.
