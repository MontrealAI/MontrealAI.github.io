Supported claims

GoalOS is an architecture and set of reference systems for compiling objectives into missions, Proof Debt, AGI Jobs, ProofBundles, Evidence Dockets, decisions, Chronicle, Validated Skills, Merkle roots and next-mission tests.

Current status

The document series, site, local applications, protocols, financial models and Commercial Proof Run 001 programme exist. Public programme metrics begin at zero.

Claims not yet earned

Paying customers for the same mission; independently verified customer outcome; repeat purchase; transferred capability with fresh improvement; recurring economics; whole-system external validation; regulated deployment; certified security; value or return; civilization-scale capacity.

Scenarios and finance

Pricing, revenue, margins, returns, allocations, market sizes and ten-year trajectories are editable management hypotheses—not forecasts, valuations, promises, investment advice or historical results.

Words reserved for proof

Avoid “guaranteed,” “100% safe,” “compliant with all laws,” “regulation-free,” “automatically profitable,” “unsupervised autonomy,” “proven recursive improvement,” “AGI achieved” or equivalents unless evidence and scope are explicit.

Authorized progression

Architecture → payment → outcome → verification → repeat purchase → transferable capability → repeatable positive contribution → recurring revenue → governed investment → measured production. Each transition requires its own receipts.
