RESEARCH, PROOF AND AUTHORITY

GoalOS sits at the convergence of enterprise agents, evaluator-driven evolution, cryptographic proof, capability governance and sovereign infrastructure.

Open the protocol notesClaim boundary

Why now

Agents are gaining context, tools, permissions and duration. Research systems generate, execute, evaluate and select modifications. Governments are structuring transparency, sovereign compute and adoption. The bottleneck becomes institutional: what deserves authority, memory, reuse and capital?

Scientific position

These developments reinforce the candidate → execution → evaluation → selection pattern. They do not demonstrate that GoalOS, general recursive improvement or large-scale capability has been achieved. GoalOS proposes the layer that makes such claims bounded, testable, revocable and falsifiable.
