NO SECRETS BEFORE CONTRACT

NDA / MSA / SOW / DPA and a protected environment are activated only where appropriate.

1Public qualification

Category, sponsor, budget, deadline, and cost of error—without sensitive details.

2Conflict and fit review

MONTREAL.AI reviews fit, conflicts, and the required reviewer route.

3Written agreement

Confidentiality, rights, security, data processing, price, proof, and responsibility.

4Protected environment

Detailed material enters only through the contractually approved channel.

Good fit

  • Identifiable accountable sponsor
  • Approved or credible budget
  • Decision with a real deadline
  • Measurable value at stake or cost of error
  • Potential access to required evidence after contract
  • Willingness to accept CONDITION, REPAIR, REJECT, or STOP

Do not send

  • Personal, customer, or employee information
  • Trade secrets, source code, or proprietary data
  • Privileged legal material
  • Credentials, passwords, keys, secrets, or vulnerabilities
  • Health, financial, biometric, or regulated data
  • Third-party information you are not authorized to disclose
