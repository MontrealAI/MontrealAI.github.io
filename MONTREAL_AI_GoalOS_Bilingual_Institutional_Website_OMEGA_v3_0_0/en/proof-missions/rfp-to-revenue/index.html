What may enter the Validated Skill Graph

  • validated company claims
  • approved security and legal statements
  • buyer requirement patterns
  • successful response structures
  • objection paths
  • pricing approval rules

Nothing enters automatically. It must be scoped, versioned, reviewed, rights-compatible, current, revocable and verifiable under the authorized root.

Reality metrics

  • response time
  • requirement coverage
  • unsupported-claim rate
  • SME hours
  • approval time
  • qualified pipeline
  • win rate
  • contract margin

Document volume and number of model calls do not constitute a customer outcome.

Professional and regulatory boundary. The system prepares responses and evidence. Authorized leaders approve every submission, price, contractual representation and customer commitment.

Proof required before claiming the product

  1. External customer and collected payment.
  2. Mission delivered under a bounded contract.
  3. Customer outcome measured with attribution limits.
  4. Independent review appropriate to the consequence level.
  5. Repeat purchase, renewal or expansion.
  6. Capability admitted and then tested on a fresh mission.
