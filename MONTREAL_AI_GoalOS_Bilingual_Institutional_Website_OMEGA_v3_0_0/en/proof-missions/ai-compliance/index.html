Document volume and number of model calls do not constitute a customer outcome.

Professional and regulatory boundary. GoalOS prepares, organizes, tests and governs evidence. Legal opinions, certifications, formal assurance and compliance decisions belong to qualified professionals.

Proof required before claiming the product

  1. External customer and collected payment.
  2. Mission delivered under a bounded contract.
  3. Customer outcome measured with attribution limits.
  4. Independent review appropriate to the consequence level.
  5. Repeat purchase, renewal or expansion.
  6. Capability admitted and then tested on a fresh mission.
