# Legal activation checklist

- Confirm French-first complete parity.
- Confirm the operating legal entity and contact details.
- Designate and publish the privacy-responsible title/contact before collecting personal information.
- Keep the public site non-confidential and static.
- Activate CASL consent records before autonomous outreach.
- Use counsel-approved clause matrices before automated contracting.
- Keep financial scenarios labeled as management hypotheses.
