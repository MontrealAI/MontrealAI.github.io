# Activation juridique et réglementaire / Legal and regulatory activation

This checklist reduces avoidable exposure; it is not legal advice and cannot guarantee immunity. / Cette liste réduit l’exposition évitable; elle ne constitue pas un avis juridique et ne garantit aucune immunité.

## Operator and publication / Opérateur et publication

- [ ] Operating entity, address, responsible persons and contact details confirmed.
- [ ] French is complete, prominent and at least as accessible as English for Quebec audiences.
- [ ] All marks, founder details, research credits and publication ownership are accurate.
- [ ] Every public claim fits the Claim Boundary and the Commercial Proof Register.

## Non-confidential public contact / Contact public non confidentiel

- [ ] Every public mission-entry surface says **NO SECRETS BEFORE CONTRACT / AUCUN SECRET AVANT CONTRAT**.
- [ ] No public page requests trade secrets, source code, credentials, keys, vulnerabilities, personal data, privileged communications, classified or export-controlled material.
- [ ] The public Mission Lab transmits and persists nothing.
- [ ] Detailed materials are accepted only after conflict/fit review, written agreement and an approved protected channel.

## Enterprise services / Services d’entreprise

- [ ] Each activated service has appropriate MSA, SOW, DPA, security, authority, acceptance, publication, IP/rights, retention, incident and termination terms.
- [ ] High-impact or regulated use is separately classified with qualified professionals and impact assessment.
- [ ] Legal opinions, formal assurance, certifications, regulated advice and fiduciary decisions remain with qualified authorized professionals.
- [ ] Customer evidence and case studies require explicit publication permission and redaction review.

## AI, Web3 and finance / IA, Web3 et finance

- [ ] No public architecture, scenario, pricing hypothesis or model is represented as a guaranteed result.
- [ ] No token, securities, investment, custody, exchange, payment, settlement or financial-advice offer is implied by the public site.
- [ ] Any future wallet or smart-contract feature receives contract audit, cryptographic review, key governance, testnet/Mainnet acceptance and incident planning.
- [ ] Any future AI interaction publishes appropriate transparency, logging, evaluation and human-authority controls.

## Jurisdiction-specific review / Revue juridictionnelle

- [ ] Quebec language and private-sector privacy obligations reviewed against the live facts.
- [ ] Canadian privacy, competition, consumer, employment, tax, export, sanctions and anti-corruption issues reviewed where applicable.
- [ ] EU AI/data/consumer requirements reviewed before targeting or serving affected users.
- [ ] Procurement, sectoral and professional rules reviewed for every activated mission class.
