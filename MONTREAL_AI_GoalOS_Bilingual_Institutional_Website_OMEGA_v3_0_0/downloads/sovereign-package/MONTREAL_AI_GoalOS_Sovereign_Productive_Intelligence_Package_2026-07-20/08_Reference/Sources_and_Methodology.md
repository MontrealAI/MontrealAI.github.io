# Sources and Methodology

## Purpose

This package translates the MONTREAL.AI / GoalOS institutional thesis into a board-ready, investor-ready and execution-ready operating system. It is intentionally designed at two levels:

- **Strategic architecture:** a global institution capable of converting intelligence into proof, proof into protected capability, capability into capital and capital into greater productive capacity.
- **Commercial proof:** one narrow, premium, falsifiable enterprise mission whose receipts determine whether the next layer deserves funding and authority.

## Evidence hierarchy

The package distinguishes five evidence states:

1. **Architecture** - a coherent design, but not customer proof.
2. **Commercial payment** - a customer has assigned economic relevance.
3. **Accepted outcome** - a defined mission has reached a contractual terminal state.
4. **Independent verification** - a separated reviewer can support or falsify material claims.
5. **Fresh transfer** - a rights-cleared capability improves a new mission under equal or harder constraints.

Only the fifth state supports a claim of bounded institutional improvement.

## Financial methodology

The companion workbook is a **management scenario architecture, not a forecast or valuation opinion**. It contains:

- editable premium product pricing;
- unit economics and gross-margin floors;
- customer value and ROI cases;
- ten-year revenue, expense, liquidity and financing scenarios;
- Treasury allocation policy;
- a Safe Verified Productive Capacity index;
- proof gates and valuation-preparedness stages.

Blue cells are editable hardcoded assumptions. Green cells link to other worksheets. Black cells are calculations. Zeros are formatted as dashes; negative values are red and in parentheses.

The ten-year Category scenario is deliberately ambitious. It exists to expose the conditions required for large-scale outcomes, not to suggest that those outcomes are probable or already earned.

## Claim boundary

The package does not claim achieved general intelligence, unrestricted recursive improvement, guaranteed customer outcomes, legal certification, audited forecasts, a present enterprise valuation or inevitable infrastructure-scale success.

The governing doctrine is:

> **Maximum ambition. Minimum unsupported claim. One consequential mission at a time.**
