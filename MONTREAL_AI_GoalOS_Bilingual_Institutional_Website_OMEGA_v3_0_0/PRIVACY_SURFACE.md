# Public privacy surface / Surface publique de confidentialité

## Current v3 static release

The public website is intentionally data-minimized:

- no account or authentication;
- no server-submitted form;
- no behavioural analytics or advertising tracker;
- no wallet connection, payment or blockchain transaction;
- no public chatbot, personalization or external model call;
- no automated consequential decision;
- no external runtime dependency;
- no customer-data processing;
- no browser persistence of Mission Lab content.

The Business Model calculator and Mission Lab run in the browser. They use categories, ranges and synthetic management assumptions only. They do not transmit information.

## Non-confidential boundary

Do not submit trade secrets, source code, personal information, credentials, cryptographic keys, vulnerabilities, privileged communications, litigation or investigation materials, classified information, export-controlled material or third-party information you lack authority to disclose.

Detailed mission information may be exchanged only after a written agreement, scope and privacy/security review, through an approved protected channel.

## Change-control law

Any future account, form, analytics, wallet, payment, API, model call, customer-data collection, third-party embed or personalization requires a new privacy analysis, updated notice, security controls, retention rules, legal review and renewed QA before activation.
