AGI Jobs Authority Boundary

An AGI Job is a bounded work specification. It may define an objective, dependencies, evidence, acceptance tests, owner, budget and stop conditions. The browser-local reference edition cannot communicate with third parties, file applications, sign contracts, move funds, access confidential systems or perform regulated acts.

External execution

External execution requires a separately constituted operator, tool permissions, security controls, data authority, professional review where applicable, human or organizational authorization and evidence capture.

Acceptance

Completion is not acceptance. A result becomes institutional truth only after the stated evidence and acceptance criteria pass and an authorized Chronicle receipt is issued.
