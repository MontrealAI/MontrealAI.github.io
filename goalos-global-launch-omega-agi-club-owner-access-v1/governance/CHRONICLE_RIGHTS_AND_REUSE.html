Chronicle Rights and Reuse

Customer secrets and private work do not become platform memory by default. Chronicle admits only narrow, attributable, rights-cleared, scoped, versioned, current, independently challenged and revocable capability that passes a fresh successor test.

Binding-action rule

Actual functions, users, data, rights, control, communications, counterparties, jurisdictions and money flows determine legal treatment. Current official sources, exact facts, qualified responsible review and attributable evidence receipts remain required.
