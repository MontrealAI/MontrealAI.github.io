No Secrets Before Contract

The public GitHub Pages edition rejects confidential, privileged, personal, regulated, classified, credential, vulnerability and unauthorized third-party information. Sensitive work requires a separately reviewed protected environment, contract, data map, processor assessment, retention policy, security controls and incident response.

Binding-action rule

Actual functions, users, data, rights, control, communications, counterparties, jurisdictions and money flows determine legal treatment. Current official sources, exact facts, qualified responsible review and attributable evidence receipts remain required.
