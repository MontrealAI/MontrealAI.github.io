Activation is a governed transaction. A jurisdiction ranking is a discovery signal, not authorization to sell, hire, own IP, claim incentives, deploy infrastructure or raise capital.

Twelve required receipts

  1. Official-source snapshot and effective-date map
  2. Local legal perimeter memorandum
  3. Tax, permanent-establishment and indirect-tax memorandum
  4. Transfer-pricing and intercompany policy where relevant
  5. Privacy, data-flow, localization and transfer assessment
  6. Employment, worker-classification and payroll assessment
  7. AI-system and sector classification
  8. Sanctions, export-control, ownership and end-use screening
  9. Funding, procurement and assistance-interaction analysis
  10. Security, incident, records and resilience plan
  11. Insurance and contractual risk-allocation review
  12. Authorized board or executive approval with Chronicle receipt
