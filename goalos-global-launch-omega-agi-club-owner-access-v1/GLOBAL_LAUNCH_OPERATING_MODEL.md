# GoalOS Global Launch Ω — Operating Model

## System architecture

1. **Global Launch Constitution** — captures what the user intends to build, the operating facts, constraints and optimization priorities.
2. **GJFFI Ω kernel** — searches jurisdiction, funding, fiscal, procurement, infrastructure, capital and regulatory routes.
3. **Apex Launch Recommendation** — presents a non-dominated architecture and switch conditions.
4. **Verified Mission Portfolio** — decomposes the architecture into bounded AGI Jobs with evidence, authority, dependencies and acceptance tests.
5. **Transaction Shield** — blocks formation, funding, tax, IP, employment, procurement, capital and infrastructure actions until required receipts exist.
6. **Global Expansion Office Ω** — monitors architecture drift and creates successor missions.
7. **Chronicle** — preserves accepted state, evidence and lineage.

## Evidence ladder

Designed → Validated → Authorized → In execution → Completed → Realized → Monitored.

The standalone produces planning candidates only. External action requires a separately constituted operator, current official sources, verified facts, qualified review where required and explicit organizational authority.
