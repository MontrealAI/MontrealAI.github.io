# GoalOS Global Expansion Office Ω — Monitoring Protocol

## Continuous watch
Programme openings and deadlines; tax and funding interactions; sanctions/export controls; regulatory changes; compute, energy, procurement and capital opportunities.

## Weekly
Opportunity pulse, source expiry, Proof Debt and jobs requiring action.

## Monthly
Funding and capital committee, cost-object stacking, applications, runway and realized cash.

## Quarterly
Jurisdiction auction, function allocation, entity topology, infrastructure and capital-stack re-optimization.

## Annual or material event
Entity substance, transfer pricing, permanent establishment, IP, employment, insurance, governance and exit readiness.

The standalone maintains browser-local monitoring state. A production operator must implement source ingestion, change detection, professional validation, alerts, versioned snapshots and superseding Chronicle receipts.
