# QUEBEC.AI Sovereign Manifesto - Crown Ultimate Style Guide

## Visual language
- Deep sovereign navy, rich metallic gold, ivory parchment, sapphire AI light.
- Premium editorial typography, not generic corporate typography.
- Use beautiful images as art and atmosphere, never as the source of body text.
- Use actual typeset text for all official documents to avoid image-generated text defects.

## Fonts
- Headings: EB Garamond Bold / high-contrast editorial serif.
- Body: EB Garamond Regular / premium book serif.
- Labels: Inter Display SemiBold / crisp executive sans.

## Core lines
- The Manifesto makes people listen. The Proof Room makes people believe.
- Le Manifeste capte l’attention. La Proof Room fonde la confiance.
- A Québec that does not rent its future, but builds it.
- Un Québec qui ne loue pas son avenir, mais qui le construit.
