# Enterprise Participation and User Responsibility Summary

The GoalOS partner system uses affirmative, versioned contractual allocation rather than caveat-heavy public messaging. Interactive actions are receipt-gated.

- Each Participant acts solely for its own account and warrants authority to bind the represented organization.
- Each Participant bears exclusive responsibility for objectives, instructions, content, data, systems, personnel, wallets, keys, transactions, taxes, reporting, licences, compliance, security, decisions, deployment and downstream use.
- Each Participant knowingly assumes AI, software, smart-contract, network, token, market, privacy, cybersecurity, validator and irreversibility risks.
- Each Participant grants the applicable release, waiver and non-recourse and agrees, to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, to hold harmless the Protected Parties from third-party claims arising from Participant-caused matters.
- User-to-user matters remain between the relevant Participants; the non-joinder covenant keeps participant-created matters with the Participants who created or accepted them.
- Every mission pack may carry the terms version, terms hashes, represented principal, role, acceptance timestamp and protective-covenant receipt.
- Permitted professional disputes follow a particularized Claim Notice, immediate evidence preservation, private executive conference, expert determination where appropriate, confidential mediation in Montréal, Montréal-seated arbitration and Montréal courts for enforcement or non-arbitrable matters.
