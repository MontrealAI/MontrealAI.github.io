# Institutional Case Study Publication Checklist

- [ ] Status is explicit: illustrative, pilot, executed, or externally replayed.
- [ ] The case does not imply a customer, certification, or production outcome that is not evidenced.
- [ ] Objective, owner, success, failure, constraints, and prohibited actions are explicit.
- [ ] Evidence status distinguishes sourced, inferred, simulated, executed, replayed, externally validated, and unresolved claims.
- [ ] A baseline or incumbent process is present.
- [ ] Issuer and authority boundaries are visible.
- [ ] Dissent, contradiction, dispute, and negative controls are preserved.
- [ ] Human review scope, expiry, unresolved risks, and revocation are recorded.
- [ ] Settlement and memory promotion are independently authorized.
- [ ] Falsification conditions and delayed outcomes are defined.
- [ ] The public/private proof boundary has been reviewed.
- [ ] Replay instructions or scoped audit access exist.
