Operational boundaries

  • The complete machine is browser-local and static. It does not itself sign contracts, charge customers, move funds, guarantee customers, or establish legal compliance.
  • Current direct *.club.agi.eth ownership activates the normal member experience. Public static source remains inspectable and can be saved after legitimate delivery; this is entitlement gating, not confidential DRM.
  • Any paid engagement requires a separate written scope, pricing, privacy, security, and responsibility allocation.
  • Do not place confidential, privileged, health, financial-account, employment, biometric, export-controlled, classified, or customer-sensitive information in the public/static tool.
