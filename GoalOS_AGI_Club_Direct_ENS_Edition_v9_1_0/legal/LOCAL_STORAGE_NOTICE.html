Cookies, Local Storage, and Browser State

Essential local state, service-worker cache, deletion, and the boundary for future analytics or forms.

No advertising cookies or analytics are included by default. The application uses browser storage only for essential sessions, member convenience, and user-created local work.

Storage used

  • sessionStorage: short-lived signed membership session.
  • localStorage: remembered club label, Money Machine plans, proof-run records, preferences, and event history.
  • Downloaded files: exports intentionally saved by the user.
  • Service-worker cache: static assets for reliability/offline use where supported.

How to delete

Use End Session, reset controls, browser site-data settings, or clear storage for the deployment origin. Removing local data does not remove public Ethereum, ENS, Git, IPFS, gateway, email, or third-party records.

Operator additions

If an operator adds analytics, support, forms, checkout, CRM, or advertising, it must update the privacy notice, identify purposes and third parties, obtain legally required consent, and provide appropriate controls before collection.
