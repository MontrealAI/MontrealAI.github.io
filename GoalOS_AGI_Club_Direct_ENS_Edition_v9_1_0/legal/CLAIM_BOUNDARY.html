Claim Boundary and Evidence Status

What this release supports, what still requires external proof, and which shortcuts are prohibited.

Claim discipline is part of the product. Architecture, implementation, controlled demonstration, real customer evidence, independent review, and externally reproduced improvement are different evidence states.

Supported statements

  • The static application can form offers, pipeline assumptions, sales drafts, proof-producing delivery structures, forecasts, local Proof Run records, and exports.
  • Current direct ENS ownership can activate the normal member experience through MetaMask and Ethereum Mainnet reads.
  • The complete Money Machine is browser-local and no activation transaction is required.
  • The system includes explicit approval gates and failure states.

Statements requiring real evidence

  • Three real paying customers, measurable customer value, positive contribution margin, repeat purchase, market leadership, or predictable acquisition.
  • Independent validation, production security, legal compliance, audit/certification, safe recursive improvement, or real second-mission capability compounding.
  • Token value, liquidity, return, yield, treasury value, or investment performance.

Prohibited shortcuts

Do not state or imply “100% safe,” “fully legally protected,” “guaranteed revenue,” “passive income,” “risk-free,” “regulation-free,” “certified,” “audited,” “ASI achieved,” “fully decentralized” without precise evidence, or equivalent absolutes. Synthetic examples must remain visibly synthetic.
