Web3, Wallet, ENS, and Static-Hosting Risk Disclosure

Plain-language risks of MetaMask, Ethereum Mainnet, ENS, IPFS, static delivery, AI, and commercial use.

Activation is gasless, but Web3 risk remains. Never approve a transaction or token permission merely to activate membership. The legitimate activation message expressly says it is not a transaction, approval, payment, or asset transfer.

Wallet risks

Private-key loss, phishing, malicious extensions, compromised devices, fake support, address poisoning, clipboard replacement, and signing deceptive messages can cause irreversible loss. The operator will never ask for a seed phrase or private key.

ENS risks

Names may be transferred, replaced, wrapped, unwrapped, expired, resolver-modified, or affected by parent controls and contract changes. A visible name does not prove identity, reputation, solvency, authority, or legality beyond the onchain ownership fact checked.

Ethereum and smart-contract risks

RPC errors, chain congestion, reorgs, gas volatility, client bugs, contract vulnerabilities, upgrade/admin powers, oracle/indexer errors, and unexpected interactions may affect results. Any optional transaction must be independently reviewed.

IPFS and gateway risks

A CID proves content identity, not availability, truth, legality, confidentiality, or safety. Gateways may log access, censor, transform URLs, or become unavailable. Pin important content redundantly and verify hashes/CIDs independently.

Static-source boundary

GitHub Pages and IPFS publish files. JavaScript delivered to a browser can be inspected and saved. The direct-ENS gate controls the intended member experience and entitlement; it does not provide unbreakable code secrecy. Confidential assets require separately reviewed encryption and key-governance systems.

Commercial and AI risks

AI and automated planning can be wrong, biased, incomplete, manipulated, or stale. Customer demand, price, conversion, delivery, margin, and repeat purchase are uncertain. Never treat forecasts or generated language as financial advice or guaranteed results.
