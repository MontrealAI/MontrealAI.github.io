Accessibility Statement

A keyboard, mobile, reduced-motion, and plain-language accessibility commitment.

Important. This document is production-oriented drafting, not a substitute for deployment-specific advice from qualified counsel or accessibility, security, privacy, tax, employment, or regulatory professionals.

Design target

The release targets keyboard operation, visible focus, responsive layouts, semantic headings, readable contrast, reduced-motion preferences, mobile touch targets, and plain-language status messages.

Known boundary

Complex planning surfaces, MetaMask prompts, spreadsheets, and third-party wallet or gateway interfaces may not satisfy every assistive-technology need. The production operator should test against WCAG 2.2 AA and applicable accessibility law before claiming conformance.

Feedback and accommodation

Report barriers or request a reasonable alternative at secretariat@montreal.ai, identifying the page, browser, assistive technology, and requested accommodation.
