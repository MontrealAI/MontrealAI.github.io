# Mission

## Mission name

AI‑First Executive Intelligence Briefing

## Mission objective

Transform an executive briefing workflow into a repeatable AI‑First capability loop.

## Audience

Executives, enterprise leaders, journalists, partners, reviewers, and strategic stakeholders.

## Success criteria

A successful output should be:

```text
Clear
Useful
Evidence-backed
Risk-aware
Executive-ready
Reusable
Reviewable
```

## Inputs

```text
Public enterprise question
Public market/context material
Internal strategic context where available
Constraints
Audience requirements
Decision deadline
```

## Outputs

```text
Executive briefing
Signal map
Risk and governance review
Decision options
Evidence notes
Reusable templates
Capability Archive entry
```
