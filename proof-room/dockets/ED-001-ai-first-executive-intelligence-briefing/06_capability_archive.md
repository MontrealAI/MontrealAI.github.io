# Capability Archive

## What is archived

| Capability | Why it matters | Reuse path |
|---|---|---|
| Executive briefing template | Gives future briefings a strong structure. | Reuse for new strategic questions. |
| Signal map pattern | Helps agents identify relevant strategic signals. | Reuse across sectors and enterprises. |
| Governance checklist | Makes risk review repeatable. | Reuse before executive promotion. |
| Agent role map | Lowers coordination cost. | Reuse in future missions. |
| Evidence Docket pattern | Makes future work easier to inspect. | Reuse for every proof case. |

## Compounding principle

The first cycle creates useful outputs.

The second cycle starts with a stronger base.

Over time, the enterprise develops an internal library of proven ways to think, work, decide, and govern.
