# Validation and governance

## What validators check

```text
Is the output clear?
Is it useful for executives?
Are claims supported?
Are uncertainties stated?
Are risks identified?
Are recommendations actionable?
Is confidential information protected?
Can the process be reused?
```

## Governance controls

```text
Bound the mission.
Separate drafting from validation.
Keep evidence notes.
Store reusable capabilities.
Escalate sensitive decisions to human authority.
```

## Promotion rule

A capability is archived only when it is useful, reusable, and safe to preserve.
