# Validation and Governance

## Review gates

1. Mission is clear.
2. Scope is bounded.
3. Claims are separated from evidence.
4. Risks and limitations are visible.
5. Outputs are useful for executive decision-making.
6. Reusable assets are captured.
7. Next-cycle improvements are documented.

## Governance standard

No important claim is promoted unless it has a matching evidence object, review note, or clearly marked future burden.

This protects the brand because it makes the Proof Room confident, not careless.
