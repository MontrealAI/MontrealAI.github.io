AGI.Eth Namespace Grammar

Main grammar

<entity>.(<env>.)<role>.agi.eth
role ∈ {club, agent, node}
env optional: alpha, x, …

Examples

alice.club.agi.eth
alice.alpha.club.agi.eth
helper.agent.agi.eth
helper.alpha.agent.agi.eth
gpu01.node.agi.eth
gpu01.alpha.node.agi.eth

Sovereigns

<sovereign>.agi.eth
env.agi.eth (alpha.agi.eth, x.agi.eth)
<business>.env.agi.eth (ops.alpha.agi.eth)

Scope rule

entity.env.role.agi.eth is recognized and
developed only within env.agi.eth.

Official environment package

env.agi.eth
env.agent.agi.eth
env.node.agi.eth
env.club.agi.eth

Aliases

agent.env.agi.eth
node.env.agi.eth
club.env.agi.eth

Aliases are env-local and not official by
default unless whitelisted.
