AGI.Eth Namespace Grammar

<entity>.( <env>. )<role>.agi.eth

role ∈ {club, agent, node}; env optional: alpha, x, …

alice.club.agi.eth | alice.alpha.club.agi.eth helper.agent.agi.eth | helper.alpha.agent.agi.eth gpu01.node.agi.eth | gpu01.alpha.node.agi.eth
<sovereign>.agi.eth env.agi.eth → alpha.agi.eth | x.agi.eth <business>.env.agi.eth → ops.alpha.agi.eth

Scope rule: entity.env.role.agi.eth is recognized and developed only within env.agi.eth.

Official package: env.agi.eth · env.agent.agi.eth · env.node.agi.eth · env.club.agi.eth Aliases: agent.env.agi.eth · node.env.agi.eth · club.env.agi.eth

Aliases are env-local and not official by default unless whitelisted.
