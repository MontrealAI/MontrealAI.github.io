# GoalOS Evidence Docket - Strategic Partnership Diligence

> Illustrative partner demonstration. Not an external validation, production certification, legal opinion, financial representation or investment recommendation.

## Objective
Determine whether to enter a bounded AI infrastructure partnership pilot.

## Governed Decision State
**Proceed with a 30-day sandbox pilot - subject to four closing conditions.**

The evidence supports strategic and technical fit for a reversible pilot. Production deployment, broad data access and auto-renewal remain blocked.

## Claims matrix

### C-01 - The integration is technically feasible
- **State:** PASS
- **Evidence / limitation:** Architecture map, API compatibility test and sandbox integration plan.
- **Authority consequence:** May proceed to sandbox build.

### C-02 - Security controls are adequate for production
- **State:** BLOCKED
- **Evidence / limitation:** Control narrative exists; independent attestation and incident evidence are missing.
- **Authority consequence:** Production access remains prohibited.

### C-03 - A restricted sandbox can be operated safely
- **State:** PASS
- **Evidence / limitation:** No production data, scoped credentials, monitoring and kill switch.
- **Authority consequence:** Pilot authority granted with scope.

### C-04 - Data residency is compatible
- **State:** CONDITIONAL
- **Evidence / limitation:** Proposed regions align; complete subprocessor and data-flow map is pending.
- **Authority consequence:** No sensitive data until closure.

### C-05 - Unit economics can be attractive
- **State:** PASS
- **Evidence / limitation:** Illustrative load-band model and sensitivity analysis.
- **Authority consequence:** Negotiate price cap and volume breakpoints.

### C-06 - The provider can support enterprise operations
- **State:** CONDITIONAL
- **Evidence / limitation:** Support model documented; two relevant references still required.
- **Authority consequence:** Pilot allowed; scale decision held.

### C-07 - Lock-in risk is controlled
- **State:** CONDITIONAL
- **Evidence / limitation:** Export path exists; contract and technical exit test remain incomplete.
- **Authority consequence:** Require portability and termination clauses.

### C-08 - The partnership is strategically aligned
- **State:** PASS
- **Evidence / limitation:** Complementarity map, executive objectives and capability gap analysis.
- **Authority consequence:** Advance to joint pilot charter.

### C-09 - All legal and regulatory issues are resolved
- **State:** REVIEW
- **Evidence / limitation:** Operational facts mapped; jurisdiction-specific legal review remains outside the system.
- **Authority consequence:** Counsel gate before production terms.

### C-10 - A 30-day pilot is reversible and decision-useful
- **State:** PASS
- **Evidence / limitation:** Milestones, acceptance tests, day-14 checkpoint and rollback plan.
- **Authority consequence:** Proceed with controlled pilot.

## Open Proof Debt
1. Independent security control evidence
2. Complete data-flow and subprocessor map
3. Two reference calls with comparable customers
4. Documented rollback rehearsal and commercial exit

## Contradictions
1. Provider markets “production ready,” while independent assurance is not yet supplied.
2. Low headline price conflicts with higher support and observability costs at enterprise scale.
3. Fast integration claim conflicts with custom identity and data-residency requirements.

## Closing conditions
1. Independent security attestation or equivalent control evidence
2. Approved data-flow and residency map
3. Two relevant enterprise reference calls
4. Rollback rehearsal, capped budget and explicit termination rights

## Chronicle
ADMIT WITH SCOPE. Pilot authority is granted. Production authority remains blocked.
