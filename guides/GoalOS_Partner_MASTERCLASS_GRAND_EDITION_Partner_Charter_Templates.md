### Partner contributions

- objective and decision context;

- success and failure criteria;

- privacy, legal, security, and public-communication constraints;
- timely review and challenge decisions.

### GoalOS contributions

- Mission Contract and claims matrix;
- Proof Debt register;
- custom AGI Job contracts;
- proof-producing execution and ProofBundle packaging;
- Evidence Docket and verifier record;
- risk and cost ledgers;
- executive brief and Action Graph;
- Chronicle recommendation;
- scoped capability candidate and rollback;
- optional typed root packet;
- Mission 2 preregistration and comparison.

### 30/60/90 evidence milestones

**Day 30 - Mission and proof map**

- Mission Contract frozen;
- major claims classified;
- Proof Debt prioritized;
- data boundary and reviewer route approved;
- first Evidence Docket index available.

**Day 60 - Governed decision state**

- required proof jobs completed or explicitly blocked;
- replay and reviewer path completed;
- Chronicle decision recorded;
- challenge and rollback state visible;
- scoped capability candidate packaged where admitted.

**Day 90 - Mission 2**

- held-out task executed under equal constraints;
- Fresh Control, Raw Memory, Validated Skill, and relevant ablation compared;
- delayed outcome and independent replay recorded where required;
- expansion, repair, hold, or termination decision made.

### Kill criteria

- no bounded decision;
- no access to material evidence;
- reviewer independence cannot be established for the selected proof level;
- private-data boundary cannot be enforced;
- irreversible action required before validation;
- unsupported public-claim pressure;
- no credible Mission 2 comparator;
- critical safety, legal, or security concern;
- unauthorized drift or governance bypass.

### Expansion gates

Expansion requires all applicable gates:

- reviewer-ready Evidence Docket;
- material Proof Debt retired or explicitly blocked;
- valid Chronicle decision;
- exact allowed scope and excluded uses;
- challenge window and rollback readiness;
- verifiable root/commitment where used;
- positive persistent Mission 2 result without greater risk;
- external replay/delayed outcome when policy requires it;
- separately approved production, legal, security, and custody gates.

### Communication law

Public communication must identify:

- the environment and constraints;
- what was demonstrated;
- what remains pending;
- the reviewer and independence posture;
- what the proof establishes and does not establish;
- the exact blocked claims.

## Template B - Board Resolution to Authorize a Founding Proof Mission

**Resolution title:** Authorization of a bounded GoalOS Founding Proof Mission

The board or authorized committee resolves that:

1. The institution will sponsor the bounded objective: **{{objective}}**.
2. The decision to support is: **{{decision}}**.
3. The mission is exploratory and evidence-producing; it does not itself authorize production deployment, external publication, fund movement, legal reliance, or irreversible action.
4. The Mission Contract must freeze scope, proof level, risk, budget, tools, data, reviewers, privacy, challenge, rollback, and done condition before material execution.
5. The designated decision owner is **{{owner}}**.
6. The designated reviewer is **{{reviewer}}**, subject to effective-control and conflict disclosure.
7. The maximum authorized mission budget is **{{budget}}** and the initial period is **{{window}}**.
8. Any material claim without sufficient evidence remains Proof Debt or blocked.
9. No candidate capability may influence future work without a valid Chronicle decision and applicable scope, freshness, replay, validation, root, challenge, and rollback conditions.
10. The team must preregister a fresh-task Mission 2 comparison before Mission 1 results are used to justify a compounding claim.
11. The final board packet must contain the Evidence Docket, reviewer verdict, risk/cost ledgers, decision state, Action Graph, rollback, claim boundary, and next proof.
12. The board retains the right to repair, hold, reject, quarantine, revoke, retire, or terminate the mission.
