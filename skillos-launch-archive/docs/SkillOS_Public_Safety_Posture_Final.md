# SkillOS Public Safety and Integrity Posture

## Purpose

This archive is designed for public viewing, meeting use, and long-term preservation.

## Public boundary

This public archive contains no private customer data, no private commercial terms, no financing materials, no confidential internal strategy, and no sensitive internal arrangements. Current metrics are reference-workflow results, not audited customer results or guarantees of future outcomes.

## Claims standard

Use:

- open-source proof
- reference workflow
- reproducible proof
- controlled environment
- measured improvement
- human approval

Avoid:

- guaranteed outcomes
- audited customer ROI unless actually audited
- passive income language
- high-impact automated decisioning
- financial, legal, medical, employment, or credit advice claims

## Human oversight

Pilot 001 uses the rule:

```text
Agent drafts. Human approves. Nothing is sent automatically.
```

## Final posture

Bold, public, credible, and careful.
