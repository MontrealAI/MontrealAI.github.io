Operating architecture

The system in four functions.

1

Identity

Direct name and current control.

2

Write

Authorization to submit within its candidate namespace.

3

Admission

Only Chronicle can make capability canonical.

4

Use

A precise entitlement is required for each skill and operation.

Direct rule

alice.agent.agi.eth may be a direct identity. x.alice.agent.agi.eth, agent.agi.eth and alice.club.agi.eth do not satisfy the same gate.

Current ownership

Control is re-read against current Ethereum state, including the ENS Registry or an approved Name Wrapper, its owner and expiry.

Scoped entitlements

SUBMIT_CANDIDATE, INVOKE, COMPOSE, TEACH, PACKAGE, AUDIT and other operations are granted separately with scope, policy, epoch, time and revocation.

Durable identity, governable authority

Identity may remain user-owned while capability entitlements remain in a separate policy registry that can be suspended without confiscating the name.
