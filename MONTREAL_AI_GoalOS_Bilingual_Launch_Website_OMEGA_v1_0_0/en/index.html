The institutional moment

Intelligence is advancing. Authority remains the bottleneck.

The market no longer lacks only capable models. It lacks an institution that knows what was attempted, what happened, what was proven, what can be replayed, what must be blocked and what deserves to influence the future.

01

Agents are moving from chat into workflows

Enterprise platforms now add tools, memory, permissions, action controls, approvals and administrative visibility.

02

Improvement is becoming an evaluation problem

Frontier systems generate candidates, execute them, evaluate them, select them and retain only measured improvements.

03

Regulation requires clear separation of content, decision and authority

Language, privacy, AI transparency, accountability and sector obligations must be built into operations—not appended later.
