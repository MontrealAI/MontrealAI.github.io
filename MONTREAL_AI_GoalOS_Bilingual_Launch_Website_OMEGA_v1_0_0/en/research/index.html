Why now

Enterprise agents are acquiring tools, memory, permissions and controls. Research systems are evolving skills under evaluation. Risk frameworks and transparency obligations are becoming operating practices. GoalOS unifies these axes around one question: what deserves authority, memory and capital?

Scientific posture

Recent work strengthens the importance of the candidate → execution → evaluation → selection pattern. It does not demonstrate that GoalOS, general recursive improvement or civilization-scale trajectories have been achieved. GoalOS proposes the institution that makes such claims testable, falsifiable, reviewable and bounded.
