No application-level collection by design

The public site uses no account, profile, server form, wallet, payment, advertising tag, pixel, behavioural analytics, A/B test, cross-site identifier or tracking system. Local JavaScript is limited to navigation and interface behaviour.

GitHub Pages hosting

GitHub, your browser, network provider, operating system and linked third parties may process technical data such as IP address, requested resource, timestamp, user agent, referrer, diagnostics and security logs under their own policies. MONTREAL.AI does not control all such processing.

Email and voluntary communications

When you use a mailto link, your email client transmits the message to the recipient. Do not send sensitive data before receiving an approved channel and appropriate contractual terms. Enterprise communications may be retained under applicable duties, security policies and schedules.

Downloads and local execution

Downloaded HTML applications may store data locally on your device as documented. The public site does not receive that state. You are responsible for the device, backups, deletion and any voluntary export.

Rights and requests

Rights vary by jurisdiction. For a request concerning information held by MONTREAL.AI, email secretariat@montreal.ai with enough detail to identify the relevant relationship without sending unnecessary secrets. Reasonable identity verification may be required.

Future services

Any service collecting personal information must publish a specific notice defining purposes, authority, categories, providers, transfers, retention, security, incidents and rights. This notice does not automatically govern a future platform, Proof Mission or customer integration.
