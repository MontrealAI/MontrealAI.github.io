The site qualifies; only a written contract authorizes the mission, data, systems and authority.

Public version v1.0.0 · Effective 20 July 2026 · Mandatory law and actual facts prevail. Independent review required before commercial or regulated use.

No contract through browsing or email

A visit, download, conversation, proposal, email or registration does not form a Proof Mission. Engagement begins only with a signed written agreement and, where relevant, purchase order or payment.

Minimum contract documents

MSA, statement of work, acceptance criteria, role matrix, budget, deadline, data, intellectual property, confidentiality, data-protection schedule, security schedule, subprocessors, location, retention, incidents, publication, insurance and liability allocation.

Human authority

Unless specifically agreed, GoalOS prepares, coordinates, tests and recommends. The customer retains final authority over deployment, contract, spending, regulated decisions, action affecting people, publication, settlement and production change.

Customer data

No customer data is used to train, enrich a cross-customer graph, publish a Docket or create reusable capability without contractual basis and explicit rights. Data, secrets, rights and restrictions must be classified before access.

Independent verification

A separated reviewer is independent only when interests, mandate, data, tools, compensation and conflicts are managed. Scope and verdict limitations must be written.

Outcome and payment

An engagement does not guarantee a favourable decision. Possible states include CONDITION, REPAIR, NARROW, PIVOT, REJECT, QUARANTINE and STOP. Payment, acceptance, licensing, settlement and Chronicle admission are distinct events.

Regulated or critical deployment

Health, finance, employment, insurance, justice, biometrics, children, safety, defence, critical infrastructure and other high-impact uses require separate scoping, qualified experts, impact assessment, assurance and controls required by law and customer.
