ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Bilingual, keyboard-operable, reduced-motion experience designed toward WCAG 2.2 AA.

§
Public version v1.0.0 · Effective 20 July 2026 · Mandatory law and actual facts prevail. Independent review required before commercial or regulated use.

Target

The site targets WCAG 2.2 AA: semantic structure, skip link, keyboard navigation, visible focus, contrast, resizable text, responsive content, declared languages and reduced-motion preference.

Limitations

PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations, historic images or third-party artifacts may have limitations. Alternative versions or assistance may be requested.

Report a barrier

Email secretariat@montreal.ai with the URL, browser, assistive technology and description. Do not include sensitive data. MONTREAL.AI will seek a reasonable solution without promising a universal timeline.

Continuous improvement

Accessibility is a publication gate. QA reports check headings, language, alternative text, links, mobile overflow, keyboard navigation and reduced motion.
