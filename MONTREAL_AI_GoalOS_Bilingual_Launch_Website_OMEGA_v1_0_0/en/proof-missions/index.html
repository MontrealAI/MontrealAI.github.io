Operating architecture

The system in four functions.

1

Real sponsor

An accountable executive, a decision and sufficient access to evidence.

2

Cost of error

A mistake expensive enough to justify proof.

3

Hard deadline

A decision that must be made now, not an indefinite exploratory workshop.

4

Institutional courage

Willingness to accept CONDITION, REPAIR, REJECT, PIVOT or STOP.

The purchasable question

Should we deploy, acquire, scale, continue funding, narrow, repair or stop this AI initiative—and what evidence is required before the decision deserves authority?

Deliverables

Mission Contract, claim register, Proof Debt, AGI Jobs, executable tests, Evidence Docket, risk register, independent verdict, decision state, action and rollback plan, and Chronicle decision.

Founding commercial hypothesis

A bounded offer from US$15,000 for founding partners, ten business days and one objective. High-assurance deployment, acquisition or board-level missions are priced against value at stake and cost of error.

Possible decisions

PROCEED / CONDITION / REPAIR / NARROW / PIVOT / REJECT / QUARANTINE / STOP. A negative decision can create more value than a premature launch.
