Standards Alignment and Non-Endorsement

GoalOS is designed to complement established AI risk-management, evaluation, cybersecurity, assurance, and critical-infrastructure practices. Alignment does not imply endorsement, accreditation, certification, governmental status, or legal compliance by itself.

Reference alignment

The architecture is designed to interoperate conceptually with approaches such as the NIST AI Risk Management Framework and the NIST Generative AI Profile, while adding Mission Contracts, Proof Debt, Evidence Dockets, Chronicle-gated memory, Mission 2 transfer, challenge, and rollback.

  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework
  • NIST Generative AI Profile

No external authority is represented as having reviewed, adopted, certified, endorsed, or designated GoalOS.
