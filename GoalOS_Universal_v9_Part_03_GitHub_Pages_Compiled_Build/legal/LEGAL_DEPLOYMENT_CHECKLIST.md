# Legal Deployment Checklist

- Confirm publisher/operator legal names, addresses, contact channels, and authority.
- Obtain Québec/Canada and target-market counsel review.
- Confirm English/French presentation and consumer-language requirements.
- Verify legal document version, effective date, and SHA-256 manifest.
- Ensure the MetaMask message uses the same legal version and bundle hash.
- Confirm no revenue, profit, token-value, certification, AGI/ASI, safety, or compliance guarantee appears.
- Confirm membership sales/refunds are governed by a separate reviewed agreement.
- Document RPC, MetaMask, GitHub Pages, IPFS, ENS, and support-provider data flows.
- Complete accessibility, privacy, security, incident-response, sanctions/export, and insurance review.
- Preserve release checksums, source commit, deployed CID/Pages URL, and acceptance-test record.
