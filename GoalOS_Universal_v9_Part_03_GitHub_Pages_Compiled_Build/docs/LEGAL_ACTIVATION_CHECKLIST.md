# Legal Activation Checklist

- [ ] Confirm the exact operating entity, addresses, contacts, and jurisdictions.
- [ ] Obtain counsel review of Terms, Privacy, Enterprise Addendum, Professional Services Terms, digital-asset language, limitation/indemnity, governing law, and French-language obligations.
- [ ] Confirm actual data flows, cookies/local storage, analytics, wallet SDK, RPCs, support tools, pinning services, and subprocessors.
- [ ] Finalize the MSA, SOW, DPA, NDA, reviewer agreement, order form, and service-level schedule for the actual offering.
- [ ] Confirm consumer, privacy, securities/commodities, AML/sanctions, tax, employment, professional-services, accessibility, marketing, and sector-specific requirements.
- [ ] Review every public performance, safety, AGI/ASI, financial, token, valuation, certification, and customer claim against the Claim Boundary and Exclusion Register.
- [ ] Generate and record the final legal-bundle hash.
- [ ] Preserve approval evidence and identify the authority permitted to publish or deploy.

No generic legal package can guarantee complete protection.
