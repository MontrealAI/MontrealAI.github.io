# Release Notes — Masterpiece Institutional Edition v3.0

## Release identity

- **Release:** `GOS-PM-MASTERPIECE-2026.07-v3.0`

## What changed

Version 3.0 synthesizes the strongest components of the Grand, APEX, Sovereign and Production Showcase editions while preserving every source experience intact.

### New Masterpiece launcher

- adaptive session composer for six delivery durations;
- audience, objective, risk, evidence-maturity and outcome routing;
- generated run of show with completion state and Markdown/JSON export;
- four coordinated front doors rather than one overloaded interface;
- 18-module curriculum map;
- three-lane evidence taxonomy;
- dynamic RSI gate laboratory;
- AI / AGI Node Council lenses;
- commercial-ladder and Deal Architect;
- receipt-bound participation using the exact v2.1 terms identifiers and hashes;
- fully offline operation and responsive desktop/mobile layout.

### New Masterpiece Executive Deck

A 32-slide synthesis deck with speaker notes covering category creation, Governed Decision States, proof operations, Evidence Dockets, evidence maturity, validation, Chronicle, the Validated Skill Graph, privacy, bonded authority, governed RSI, Mission 2, the commercial ladder, the first pilot and the calibrated participation framework.

### New Masterpiece Operating Guide

A 35-page editable guide covering every delivery mode, exact presenter language, proof objects, validation, capability memory, RSI, REAL-001, evidence taxonomy, commercial conversion, participation, diligence, objections and the final partner close.

### Combined presentation book

A 67-page mixed-format PDF combines the 32-slide executive deck and 35-page operating guide.

### Calibrated participation wording

The partner-facing framework uses:

> **Hold harmless & non-joinder**  
> The Participant agrees to hold harmless the Protected Parties for Participant-caused matters and keep user-to-user matters between the relevant Participants.

The calibrated wording is used consistently across the launcher, presentation and operating guide.
