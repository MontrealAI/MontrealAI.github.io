# GoalOS Partner Pilot Blueprint

## Purpose
Convert one consequential, reversible objective into a reviewer-ready Governed Decision State.

## Duration
15 business days.

## Partner inputs
- Named executive sponsor
- Decision and deadline
- Accessible evidence and subject-matter contacts
- Independent reviewer
- Defined public/private boundary
- Explicit prohibited actions and rollback conditions
- Authorized enterprise participation receipt

## Operating sequence
1. Freeze the Mission Contract.
2. Convert unsupported claims into Proof Debt.
3. Create mission-specific AGI Jobs.
4. Return structured ProofBundles.
5. Assemble the Evidence Docket.
6. Route independent validation and repair.
7. Issue the Chronicle verdict.
8. Package the Action Graph and reusable capability passport.

## Deliverables
- Mission Contract
- Claims matrix and contradiction register
- Proof Debt register
- AGI Jobs and ProofBundles
- Evidence Docket and verifier report
- Risk and cost ledgers
- Executive brief and decision deck
- Action Graph and rollback plan
- Chronicle verdict
- Reusable capability passport

## Participant responsibility allocation
The mission sponsor and represented organization retain exclusive responsibility for the objective, evidence, instructions, approvals, content, data, systems, personnel, wallets, taxes, licences, regulatory compliance, security, deployment and downstream use. The versioned acceptance receipt, release, hold-harmless, participant-to-participant, non-joinder, evidence-preservation and Montréal dispute terms travel with the mission package.
