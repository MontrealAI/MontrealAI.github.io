Enterprise participation framework

User-directed. User-controlled. User-responsible.

Every protected function is conditioned on a versioned acceptance receipt that binds authority, responsibility, risk, release, hold-harmless, participant-to-participant matters, non-joinder and the professional dispute protocol to the mission evidence package.

Enterprise responsibility allocation

Commercial and professional participation only
ACCEPTANCE REQUIRED
Participant authorityThe accepting person warrants full authority to act for and bind the represented organization, direct the mission and approve the resulting actions.
Exclusive responsibilityThe Participant bears all responsibility for objectives, instructions, content, data, systems, personnel, wallets, keys, taxes, reporting, licences, compliance, security, decisions, deployment and downstream use.
Risk assumption & releaseThe Participant accepts AI, software, smart-contract, network, token, cybersecurity, privacy, market, validator and irreversibility risks and grants the applicable release, waiver and non-recourse.
Hold harmless & non-joinderThe Participant agrees to hold harmless the Protected Parties for Participant-caused matters and keeps user-to-user matters between the relevant Participants.
Contractual gate. No mission execution, validation, settlement, reuse or export is authorized without an effective participation receipt containing the terms version, terms hash, represented principal, role, timestamp and acceptance record.
