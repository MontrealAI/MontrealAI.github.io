# GoalOS Partner Showcase v2.1 - Final Production Partner Edition

## Purpose

This release finalizes the GoalOS Partner Showcase as a presentation-ready, receipt-gated proof system for high-calibre enterprise, institutional and strategic discussions.

## Material refinement

The prior public-facing **Defence, indemnity & non-joinder** formulation has been replaced with:

> **Hold harmless & non-joinder**  
> You agree to hold harmless the Protected Parties for Participant-caused matters and keep user-to-user matters between the relevant Participants.

The revision is deliberately clearer and more proportionate while preserving the central allocation: every Participant acts for its own account, remains responsible for its own activity and keeps user-to-user matters with the relevant Participants.

## Contractual calibration

- No default duty to assume control of a defence or advance legal or expert costs.
- No gross-up mechanism.
- Hold-harmless protection is tied to Participant-caused and third-party matters.
- User-to-user matters remain between the relevant Participants.
- Non-joinder is calibrated to preserve lawful process, separately agreed direct claims and non-waivable rights.
- Exclusive Participant responsibility, assumption of risk, release, non-recourse, tax allocation, evidence preservation and the Montréal professional dispute protocol remain in force to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law.

## Production system

- Mandatory versioned **Enterprise Participation Receipt** before mission execution, validation, settlement demonstration, reuse or export.
- French-first delivery and subsequent contract-language election recorded in the receipt.
- Exact terms version and English, French and bilingual hashes bound to the interface, receipt and mission exports.
- Represented organization, Participant role, authority representation, acceptance timestamp and protective covenants travel with the evidence package.
- Complete boardroom system: interactive demonstrator, executive deck, executive pre-read, sample Governed Decision State, presenter runbook, bilingual participation instrument, sample exports and launch center.
