# Revision Memorandum - GoalOS Partner Showcase v2.1

## Final decision

The earlier public-facing **Defence, indemnity & non-joinder** formulation has been replaced by the more proportionate and commercially suitable formulation below.

> **Hold harmless & non-joinder**  
> You agree to hold harmless the Protected Parties for Participant-caused matters and keep user-to-user matters between the relevant Participants.

## Operative effect

- The public interface, executive deck, sample decision package, runbook, receipt and mission exports now use the calibrated formulation.
- The full terms limit hold-harmless protection to Participant-caused and third-party matters, subject to applicable mandatory law.
- The full terms do **not** create an independent duty to assume control of a defence or to advance legal or expert costs.
- The prior gross-up mechanism has been removed. Tax, withholding, reporting and recordkeeping obligations remain allocated to the Participant whose activity creates them, subject to duties imposed directly by applicable law.
- User-to-user matters remain between the relevant Participants. Non-joinder is scoped so that it does not restrict lawful process, a direct claim expressly permitted by a separate signed agreement, or a non-waivable right.
