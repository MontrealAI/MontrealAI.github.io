# GoalOS 30-Day Partner Pilot Charter

## Week 1 - Freeze authority
Mission Contract, proof level, claim boundary, privacy, risk, validators, blocked claims, baseline, rollback, Mission 2 metric.

## Week 2 - Produce proof
Custom AGI Jobs, ProofBundles, provenance, contradiction register, cost/risk ledgers, failure branches, Evidence Docket.

## Week 3 - Validate and challenge
Independent replay, AGI Node commit-reveal, falsification, scope review, Chronicle recommendation.

## Week 4 - Deliver and test transfer
Governed Decision State, Validated Skill Passport, Merkle packet, four-arm Mission 2 comparison, executive dossier.

## Deliverables
Mission Contract; Proof Debt Register; AGI Job Manifest; Evidence Docket; validator report; risk and rollback ledger; executive brief; Chronicle decision; Validated Skill Passport; Merkle packet; Mission 2 report; claim boundary.
