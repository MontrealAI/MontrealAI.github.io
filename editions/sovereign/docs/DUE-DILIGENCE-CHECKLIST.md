# GoalOS Partner Due-Diligence Checklist

- Is the objective and decision authority explicit?
- Are major claims linked to evidence or explicit uncertainty?
- Are failure branches and blocked claims preserved?
- Are validators independent by operator, model, infrastructure, keys, and incentives?
- Can another operator replay accepted results?
- Does Chronicle record scope, freshness, risk, utility, and rollback?
- Is the exact admitted state committed and tamper-detectable?
- Does Mission 2 compare against fresh and raw-memory controls under equal constraints?
- Are private methods and customer data protected?
- Are token, legal, tax, production, and empirical claims bounded?
- Are external replay and delayed outcomes completed before stronger claims are promoted?
