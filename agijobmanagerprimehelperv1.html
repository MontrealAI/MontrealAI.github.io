9) Best-practice reminders

  1. Use the same wallet for compute, approve, commit, and reveal.
  2. Do not change the application URI after you commit.
  3. Do not lose your salt. Save it in at least two places.
  4. If MetaMask shows a pending transaction, wait for it to confirm before sending the next one.
  5. If a transaction is likely to fail, use the blockers shown here instead of guessing.
