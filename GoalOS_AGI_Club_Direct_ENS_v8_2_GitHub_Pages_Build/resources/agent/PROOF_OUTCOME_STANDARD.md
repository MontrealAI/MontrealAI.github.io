# AGI Club Proof-Backed Outcome Standard

A GoalOS engagement is not complete because a model produced an answer. It is complete when the required authority objects exist and the configured gates have been resolved.

## Minimum outcome package

1. Customer objective and decision to support
2. Mission Contract
3. Claims matrix and Proof Debt Register
4. Custom AGI Job queue
5. Source provenance and ProofBundles
6. Evidence Docket
7. Contradiction, uncertainty, cost, and risk ledgers
8. Independent or required reviewer verdict
9. Governed Decision State
10. Action Graph with approvals and rollback
11. Chronicle decision
12. Validated Skill Passport, only if admission is earned
13. Mission 2 reuse test, where applicable

## Failure law

Failed proof creates repair work or a bounded stop decision. It does not create a testimonial, trusted memory, settlement authority, or capability-reuse claim.

## Public claim law

A public outcome must distinguish:

- what was attempted;
- what passed;
- what failed;
- what remains uncertain;
- what evidence can be reviewed;
- what is private;
- what is not certified or authorized;
- what future test would strengthen or falsify the claim.
