Wallet and ENS Access Notice

Exactly what MetaMask signs and how direct membership ownership is verified.

Expected member experience: enter one club label, connect the owning MetaMask account, and approve one readable, free signature. No transaction, gas payment, token approval, seed phrase, or private key is required.

Verification order

  1. The application derives the exact direct name, such as elite.club.agi.eth.
  2. It reads current Ethereum Mainnet ownership at one pinned block through the ENS Registry and, where applicable, the official Name Wrapper.
  3. Only after ownership passes does it request a five-minute MetaMask access signature.
  4. The locally recovered signer must equal the verified owner before member functionality activates.

What the signature does

It proves control of the selected MetaMask account and records informed access acceptance. The message binds the exact application URL, domain, direct club name, namehash, Ethereum Mainnet, wallet address, legal-bundle version and SHA-256 hash, random nonce, issue time, and five-minute expiry. The application locally recovers and compares the signer.

What the signature does not do

It does not transfer ETH or tokens, approve spending, delegate wallet permissions, change ENS records, renew a name, create a subscription, authorize later transactions, or give support personnel access to the wallet.

Ownership paths checked

  • Unwrapped: the ENS Registry owner of the exact namehash must equal the selected account.
  • Wrapped: the Registry must point to an explicitly configured Ethereum Mainnet Name Wrapper; both ownerOf and getData(...).owner must equal the selected account; and the recorded expiry must be present and in the future.
  • Configured wrapper: production pins the official Ethereum Mainnet Name Wrapper at 0xD4416b13d2b3a9aBae7AcD5D6C2BbDBE25686401. The Sepolia wrapper is rejected. Any other Mainnet wrapper requires explicit independent review, configuration, and controlled acceptance.

Names accepted

Only one direct label under club.agi.eth is accepted. A member may enter elite or elite.club.agi.eth. The application rejects the parent name, nested subnames, other roles, invalid normalized names, former owners after transfer, and expired wrapped names.

Receipt and privacy

After successful verification, the member may download a local JSON access receipt containing the signed message, signature, wallet, direct ENS name, ownership path, verification block, exact application URL, legal version and hash, and timestamps. The receipt is evidence of this client-side access flow, not government identity, KYC, certification, or a legal opinion. Never send a seed phrase, private key, or recovery code to anyone.
