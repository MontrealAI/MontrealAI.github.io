Accessibility Statement

The intended accessible experience and how to report barriers.

Commitment

The publisher aims for practical accessibility across keyboard, screen reader, reduced-motion, high-contrast, mobile, and zoom use. The core access sequence is labelled, keyboard-operable, and provides plain-language error messages.

Known constraints

MetaMask, wallet QR interfaces, third-party PDF/Office documents, external gateways, and embedded legacy applications may have independent accessibility limitations. Static hosting may limit server-side content negotiation and accessibility headers.

Feedback

Report barriers to secretariat@montreal.ai, including device, browser, assistive technology, page, and desired outcome.
