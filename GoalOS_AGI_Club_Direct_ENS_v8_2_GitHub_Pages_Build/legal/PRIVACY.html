Privacy Notice

Browser-local processing, public blockchain data, and third-party infrastructure.

Privacy posture: The default application is a static browser application. It does not require a GoalOS account and does not send member inputs to an application backend by default.

Information processed

  • The club label you enter, the derived full ENS name, namehash, selected wallet address, wallet signature, and current on-chain ownership data.
  • Local browser data such as the last club label, access receipt, Money Machine plans, drafts, preferences, and exports.
  • Technical network information that may be processed by MetaMask, RPC providers, GitHub Pages, IPFS gateways, browsers, or other third parties.

Purpose

Data is processed to connect the wallet, verify direct membership, display membership state, bind the access signature to the current legal bundle, activate the member interface, preserve local convenience, prevent obvious misuse, and support troubleshooting initiated by the user.

No default operator database

The static release does not include an operator-controlled account database, analytics SDK, advertising tracker, or server-side member profile. The access receipt is stored locally unless you choose to download, transmit, or share it. Public blockchain information remains public and cannot generally be erased by the publisher.

Third parties

MetaMask, Ethereum RPC endpoints, ENS contracts, GitHub, IPFS gateways, email providers, and linked services have their own privacy practices. RPC requests may reveal an IP address and the ENS node or wallet-related query. Use a trusted gateway or self-hosted RPC where stronger network privacy is required.

Local storage

Local storage is used for the last club label and access receipt. The embedded Money Machine may store plans and preferences locally. Clear the site’s storage in your browser or use the disconnect controls to remove session data. Local storage is not encrypted by default.

Retention and rights

The publisher does not control third-party or blockchain retention. Where the publisher receives a support message or separate service engagement, the applicable privacy terms and mandatory rights apply. Contact secretariat@montreal.ai for privacy requests concerning data actually controlled by the publisher.

Children

The Service is not directed to children. Do not use it if you are below the legal age required to agree to the Terms.
